What is this site?

I created this site to help me with flash cards for piano so i do not have to flip them and i can hit the notes while playing the piano as i could not find any websites which have this functionality. However, there are apps if you are more of a phone user. Remember do not look at your keyboard when playing or try not to. The point of this is to become better sight reader.
